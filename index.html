Wegovy Bootcamp 6-12 June, Radisson Blu, Sandton The Weight is over…WeGo Together…WeGo Forward….WeGo Further….WeGo…For Results Get ready for the excitement! Kicking off on June 6th, our teams plunged into the world of Wegovy, the only once-a-week GLP-1 therapy for weight loss in South Africa, backed by compelling clinical trials. With insights from experts like Cardiologist Dr. Tsebetse, we explored the critical link between cardiovascular disease and obesity, reinforcing Wegovy’s MACE benefits alongside our dedicated medical colleagues.

The action-packed week featured the dynamic 20:20:20 Wegovy strategy, in-depth competitor training, and effective objection handling. Our united Wegovy sales team, fuelled by determination, rose to the challenge, showcasing their expertise in two thrilling days of competitive selling. They emerged as true champions, ready to serve powerful punches to LASPEN and Mounjaro!
Novo Health Week Wrap-Up From May 19 to 23, 2025, Novo Nordisk hosted an enriching Novo Health Week focused on mental health and well-being. This successful event brought together participants from diverse backgrounds to engage in meaningful discussions, share insights, and promote awareness about essential mental health topics.

Highlights included a Healthy Breakfast showcasing delicious dishes prepared by our colleagues, an enlightening Online Lecture on "The Importance of Mental Health," and interactive sessions like "Walk & Talk" and "Mental Health and Stigma." Thank you to everyone who participated in making this week a true celebration of mental well-being!
